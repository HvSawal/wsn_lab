Today, there are roughly 22,536,000 registered vehicles on the roads of India. These vehicles produce various gases like NO2 , CO2 etc. because of the combustion of fuel. Besides the harmful effects on our health, they also adversely affect the environment.

The aim of this project is to show these effects. We will try to track the levels of various gases including CO2 , CO, NO2 , NO, and O2 .

The libelium waspmote board will be used, along with the gas sensor board. To show the difference in readings, values will be recorded both at a busy road as well as a non-congested area. The results will be uploaded to the cloud and can be accessed via a GUI. Basic analysis will be done on this data to show the quality of the air. This will help show the harmful effects of urban traffic and also help people avoid congested areas.

Libelium Waspmote

  • Waspmote is an open source wireless sensor platform.
  • Focused on the implementation of low consumption modes to allow the sensor nodes (“motes”) to be completely autonomous and battery powered.
  • Variable lifetime between 1 and 5 years depending on the duty cycle and the radio used.

Gas Sensor Boards

The Waspmote Gases 2.0 board has been designed to monitor environmental parameters such as temperature, humidity, atmospheric pressure and 14 different types of gases. It allows the inclusion of 7 gases sensors at the same time, the regulation of their power through a system of solid state switches and the amplification of the output signal of each one of them through a non-inverting amplification stage of a maximum gain of 101 controlled by a digital potentiometer configurable through the Inter-Integrated Circuit Bus, I2C).

Sensors

The gases which can be monitored are:

  • Carbon Monoxide - CO
  • Carbon Dioxide - CO2
  • Moleculer Oxygen - O2
  • Nitrogen Dioxide - NO2
